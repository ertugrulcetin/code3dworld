## Cursive + shadow-cljs ClojureScript REPL Integration
`npx shadow-cljs pom`

`npx shadow-cljs watch :renderer` 

`Create a remote nREPL`

`(shadow/watch :renderer)`

`(shadow/repl :renderer)`
